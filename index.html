Organization Overview

TSHEETSANO Community Based Organization & Enterprise is a community-driven organization dedicated to transforming lives affected by hunger, poverty, and unemployment through sustainable food security initiatives and organic medicinal plant development.

The organization works closely with communities to empower individuals and families by providing agricultural training, promoting food production, supporting medicinal plant cultivation, and creating economic opportunities that improve livelihoods.

Comfrey Herbal Ointment

Comfrey is an ancient healing plant.

  • Heals wounds & fractures fast – “Bone knit” herb.
  • Relieves joint & back pain – Rub on knees, hips, back.
  • Reduces swelling & insect bites – Soothes itchiness.
  • Softens rough skin – Good for cracked heels, elbows.
  • Eases muscle pain – Use after workouts.

Use: Apply a bit on affected area, massage 1–2 mins. Use 2–3x daily. Not for open wounds or ingestion.

Moringa_ and _Guava Leaf Powder

1. MORINGA
Moringa is called the “tree of life”.

  • Boosts immunity & energy – Rich in vitamins A, C, E, iron.
  • Improves digestion – Relieves stomach pain, constipation.
  • Helps with joints & skin – Reduces inflammation.
  • Good for diabetics & blood pressure (consult doctor).

Use: Add 1 tsp Moringa powder to meals or drinks.

2. GUAVA LEAF POWDER
Guava leaf has strong natural medicine.

  • Stops diarrhea & stomach pain fast – Traditional remedy.
  • Clears cough & chest – Opens airways.
  • Heals wounds – Apply powder on clean cuts.
  • Regulates blood sugar & weight – Blocks fat absorption.
  • Cleanses gut – Kills bad bacteria.

Use: Mix ½ tsp in warm water. Drink 1–2x daily max.

Sustainable Services

Our Custom Program Offerings

From ancient healing wisdom handed down generations to modern economic development platforms that rebuild families.

Traditional Healing

Dr. Tefo offers personalized holistic healing diagnostics based on ancient African medicinal wisdom, restoring health utilizing safe organic botanic remedies.

Spiritual Counseling

Compassionate emotional healing, ancestral path consulting, family system counseling, and guidance to resolve internal distress and discover peace.

Organic Medicinal Plant Cultivation

Nurturing native regional cultivars under secure greenhouse structures, supporting biological diversity and restoring endangered therapeutic assets.

Agricultural Training

Practical course programs in regenerative bio-prepping, worm castings, crop rotates, and water conservancy to transform smallholder agriculture.

Food Security Programs

Establishing collaborative feeding and vegetable programs to safeguard vulnerable children, orphans, and local families from structural food shortages.

Community Development

Integrating local labor co-operatives, promoting mutual self-reliance workshops, and securing resources for underprivileged districts.

Skills Development Training

Alternative training modules in bio-pulp crafting, basic sales bookkeeping, and sustainable nursery preservation to help individuals search careers.

Job Creation Initiatives

Securing direct avenues for local farmers and crafts guilds to distribute fresh crops to regional markets, converting labor into livable safety budgets.
